About NYUAD Marketplace

Welcome to the NYUAD Marketplace, a platform created by students, for students. Our mission is to provide a safe and convenient way for NYUAD community members to buy and sell items within our campus.

Our Vision

We aim to create a sustainable marketplace that promotes reuse and recycling within our community, while making it easy for students to find what they need and sell what they no longer use.

How It Works

  • Browse items listed by fellow students
  • Add items to your cart
  • Complete your purchase with secure payment options
  • Arrange delivery or pickup with the seller

Safety First

All transactions are verified through NYUAD NetID to ensure the safety and security of our community members.
